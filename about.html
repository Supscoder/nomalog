About Nomalog

Engineering Excellence Since 2016

Our Story

Nomalog was founded in 2016 with a vision to deliver innovative ICT solutions and consulting services to businesses across South Africa. From inception, we have been committed to engineering the future through cutting-edge technology and expert guidance.

Over the years, Nomalog has established itself as a trusted partner for organizations seeking comprehensive IT services, consulting, and technology solutions. Our expertise spans application development, software solutions, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and digital transformation.

We were once in partnership with the Ntsika Foundation, further strengthening our commitment to supporting business growth and technological advancement in South Africa.
